What is a Phantom Wallet?

Phantom Wallet is a non-custodial crypto wallet that allows you to access decentralized apps available on the Solana network. With the advancement in technology, cybercrime and digital threat to cryptocurrencies are increased and thus it became important to secure them. The Phantom wallet of the Solana blockchain allows you to trade cryptocurrency and manage all your digital assets. 

Initially, the Phantom wallet was only available as a browser extension. But later on, the company launched an app for iPhone users. You can use the Phantom app to directly access all its services. For this, you just have to download and install the wallet from its website, i.e., https://phantom.app/. After successfully installing the app on your device, you can easily deposit, transfer, stake, and swap assets on Solana using it.

How to Set Up Phantom Wallet?

Phantom Wallet is available as a simple browser extension or a browser plug-in. Therefore, it is very easy to use the wallet on a computer. Soon the company will release a mobile application for both Android and iOS devices. But for now, you can follow the below-given steps to install the service on your computer: 

  • Open your web browser in your system and go to the official website of Phantom Wallet.
  • Locate and click the “Download” button appearing on the site.
  • Now, you need to scroll and click the “Add to browser” button.
  • At last, go through the on-screen instructions and wrap the set-up process.

After successfully completing the installation process, you will notice that a plug-in is added to your browser. You can use these plug-ins to avail the services of Phantom crypto wallet. However, if you are facing a problem while adding the browser plug-in, try to identify the root cause of the issue. Go to the list of all the plugins installed on your desired browser and search for Phantom. 

How do I Access Phantom Wallet on iPhone?

Follow the below-given steps to access Phantom wallet on iPhone:

  • Firstly, visit https://phantom.app/download or open the iOS app store on your iPhone.
  • Now, choose the device on which you have to install the app. Then, you will be taken to the web store. 
  • If you have to download the Phantom app on iPhone, tap the “iPhone” button. 
  • Next, you need to wait till the app is downloaded on your iPhone. 
  • After successfully installing the app on your iPhone, it will auto-open. 
  • Now, launch the app by clicking the Phantom wallet icon appearing on the screen.

After completing the setup process, go through the Phantom wallet login steps to access your account. After successful login to your account, you can send, receive, collect NFTs, and swap tokens.

How to Recover Phantom Wallet Password?

If you forget the password of your Phantom wallet, then it is important to recover your wallet to access its services. Given below are some simple steps that you need to follow in order to recover the Phantom Wallet:

  • Click on the Phantom wallet extension to open it or launch its mobile application.
  • Now, locate and tap the ‘Import Wallet’ button.
  • Enter the 12-word seed phrase details in the respective place.
  • Go through all the details that you have entered and make sure everything is correct.
  • Create a new password for your wallet and enter it again in the respective box to confirm.
  • Now, press the ‘Recover my wallet’ button to complete the recovery process.
